Welcome

Welcome to the institutional navigator of the Electric Technocracy Community — a multilingual portal documenting the doctrines, deeds, and archives of a planetary civic transition.

Mission

Our mission is to publish, preserve, and propagate the founding instruments of Electric Technocracy in every major world language with full juridical transparency.

Electric Technocracy

Electric Technocracy is the post-monetary civic order founded on energy accounting, automated administration, and human dignity guaranteed by deed.

Age of Transition

The Age of Transition designates the historic interval in which legacy sovereignties peacefully recede before the constitutional inheritance of the planetary commons.

Juridical Singularity

The Juridical Singularity is the moment at which all legitimate legal claims converge upon a single, verifiable, planetary instrument of succession.

World Succession Deed

The World Succession Deed is the master juridical act through which planetary stewardship is transferred to the constituted Community under verified custody.
